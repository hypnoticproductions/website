
Morning Activation Protocol: 5 AM
Energy Field Expansion (5-10 minutes)
Definition: Activates your morning power field to its full span, allowing you to set the energy tone for the day with an expansive impact radius.

In Practice:

Induction:

Find a quiet, comfortable place to stand or sit. Close your eyes and take three deep, grounding breaths, focusing on the sensation of air entering and leaving your body.
With each inhale, imagine drawing in pure energy from the earth below you, filling your core. Visualize this energy pooling within you like a glowing light at your center.
Main Activity:

EFT Tapping Points: Collarbone, center of the chest.
Instructions:
Collarbone Tapping: Begin tapping gently on the collarbone points with your fingertips. With each tap, feel the energy in your core intensifying and expanding. Visualize it reaching outward, extending beyond your body in a protective, radiant sphere.
Chest Tapping: Move your tapping to the center of the chest. Imagine your energy field pushing outward even further, creating an invisible boundary around you that is both protective and empowering.
Mentally affirm with each tap: “My energy radiates with clarity and purpose. I expand to my full potential.”
Affirmation:

After finishing the tapping, place your hands gently over your heart, breathe deeply, and silently affirm: “My energy field is expansive, strong, and aligned with my highest purpose.”
Usage:

This exercise is designed to set the energetic boundary you will carry throughout the day, establishing a sense of openness and empowerment. You’ll be prepared to face the day with enhanced presence and a steady, expansive energy field.
